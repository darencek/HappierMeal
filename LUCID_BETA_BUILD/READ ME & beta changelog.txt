***** NOTES *****

- Money earned = Rest x Number of hours slept
- Rest is generated passively at 1 per 10 minutes slept
- Workshop unlocks the upgrade menu
- Tree fruits and Garden Crops grow regardless of sleeping


***** CONTROLS FOR PC *****

Game is designed to be played on Portrait touch screen devices, but works on regular computer monitors too.
- Click and drag to move the camera
- Mouse scroll to zoom in and out
- PRESS P TO EXIT THE GAME
